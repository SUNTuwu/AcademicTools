Remarks
1. To achieve the optimum immune response of the vaccine, please finish the 3-dose regimen in the designated dates.
2. For people aged 9-14 years old, a 2-dose schedule is recommended. The 2-dose schedule is the dates of the 1st and the 3rd dose. For people aged 15 years old or above, a 3-dose schedule is recommended.
3. The special fee is only available during the above schedule dates.  Clinic standard price of $1900 per dose with consultation fee will be applied out of the schedule dates.
4. The dates of the campaign may be subject to change under special circumstances and participants will be notified by email.
