Dear students,

 

Welcome to a new academic year! Hope you are enjoying your first week back to school. With the start of a new school year, Be Our Guest @HKUST is looking for committed and passionate students to be part of our team!

 

ABOUT US

Established in 2015, Be Our Guest @HKUST is a student-led initiative supported by the HKUST Office of Global Learning. We provide a platform to connect local and international students to promote cultural diversity and facilitate in-depth cultural exchange experiences.

 

What you can get from joining Be Our Guest @HKUST:

*	Develop valuable hard and soft skills relating to your team of choice
*	Gain additional exposure to cultural diversity
*	Make and work with new friends from different backgrounds and expand your social network

 

INTERESTED IN JOINING US? 

We’ve prepared an Application Package <https://drive.google.com/file/d/1o91_TZ6POriBLmNehWKbgovzWU4Z3FAI/view>  for you to learn more about us and the roles available. If you are interested in joining us, please fill in the online application form on or before 23:59 (GMT+8) 12th September 2025. A confirmation email and interview time slot will be sent to you by 17th September 2025, should you be shortlisted for an interview.
