🔔 Apply NOW - Awards for Social Good

 <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/sites/default/files/inline-images/ASG%20600x150.png> 

Make a real impact and be recognized for it! The HKUST Connect Awards for Social Good honor undergraduates committed to driving positive change. Whether you’re just starting out or are already an active changemaker, there’s an award tier for you—
👣Social Good Contributor <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/get-involved/awards-resources/service-awards/awards-for-social-good>   |  ❤️Social Good Builder <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/get-involved/awards-resources/service-awards/awards-for-social-good>   |  ✨Social Good Leader Award <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/get-involved/awards-resources/service-awards/awards-for-social-good>  
—with cash prizes up to HK$10,000 and a certificate.

 

It’s your chance to build skills, boost your CV, and create real impact alongside new friends. To qualify, join upcoming HKUST Connect events such as leadership development programs, community services, and Gear Up Training Series (GUTS) events.

 

📣 Check out the opportunities below to launch your journey to recognition and impact! ❤️‍🔥

	Calling for Ambassadors!

Do you have a social cause you’re passionate about? Have you always wanted to explore ways to make a real impact in your community?

 

Join Connect ACE! In this program, you’ll team up with like-minded peers and partner with an NGO to create a service project, mobilizing your fellow students to contribute to the community.

 

Seize this unique opportunity to transform your ideas into meaningful, lasting change!

 

#SocialGoodLeader

Apply by 14 Sep <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/development-programs/connect-ace/overview-connect-ace> 

 <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/sites/default/files/styles/newsletter_290xauto/public/2025-08/Reel%20Good%20%28290x290px%29_1.png?itok=jcAvvuEA> 

	Lights, Camera, Impact: Join the Reel Good Program!

Turn your passion into action! Through the program, you'll gain filmmaking and storytelling skills, collaborating with your peers to curate impactful videos on social issues in Hong Kong that share diverse perspectives and advocate for change.

 

Are you ready to create reels that inspire and spark change through your unique lens? Apply today!

 

#SocialGoodBuilder

Apply by 14 Sep <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/development-programs/reel-good/overview> 

 <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/sites/default/files/styles/newsletter_290xauto/public/2025-08/Sew-cial%20Club%20%28290x290px%29_3.png?itok=-A25niN-> 

	We're back as Sew-cial Club!

Join the vibrant community and unleash your craftsmanship and creative potential!

 

At Sew-cial Club, we design, prototype, and create unique products by upcycling antimicrobial fabric scraps to improve the daily lives of underserved members of our community.

 

If you’re passionate about sustainable development and making a difference, join the Club today!

 

#SocialGoodBuilder

Apply by 14 Sep <https://connect.hkust.edu.hk/development-programs/sew-cial-club/overview>
