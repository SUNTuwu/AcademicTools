Dear Students, 

 

The Center for Language Education (CLE) will be offering a series of Chinese communication courses under the Common Core curriculum in the Fall semester of 2025. Please visit the Chinese curriculum webpage <https://cle.hkust.edu.hk/courses/common-core/chinese>  for more course information. Students are expected to select a suitable Chinese course carefully according to the Guidelines <https://cle.hkust.edu.hk/courses/common-core/chinese/selection-guideline>  provided by the CLE.

 

Important Message to Final Year Students taking C-Comm

If you are going to graduate in Fall 2025 and have not yet fulfilled the Chinese Communication Common Core requirements, please make use of your early enrollment appointment slot for students of Years 4 & 5 [Tuesday 26 Aug 2025: 9.30am-10.30am / 10.30am-11.30am / 11.30am–12.30pm] during the coming course enrollment period to secure a place, in order to ensure that you can fulfil the C-Comm requirement for graduation. Places will be very tight, please plan ahead.

 

Should you have any queries, please feel free to contact us.
