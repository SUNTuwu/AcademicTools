To: All Undergraduate and Postgraduate Students

cc: All faculty and teaching staff

Dear Students,

 

A heartfelt welcome to each of you! Whether you are returning to campus or joining us for the first time, we are truly delighted to have you as part of the HKUST community. You are valued members of our community, and your presence enriches our vibrant and diverse university.

 

As we step into a new academic year starting next Monday (September 1), the campus will once again be alive with energy, curiosity, and excitement. We’re looking forward to the incredible mix of backgrounds, experiences, and talents that all of you, returning, new first-year, and exchange-in students, bring to our community.

 

At HKUST, we believe that education is not just about academic achievement, but also about personal growth, collaboration, and mutual respect. We encourage you to engage fully in campus life, support one another, and appreciate the diverse perspectives that make our university unique. Our goal is to foster an inclusive and supportive environment where everyone can thrive.

 

We are committed to open communication and are always here to listen to your ideas and concerns. Should you have any questions or need guidance, please feel free to reach out to us, your home department/division, or consult the resources available on the Dean of Students’ Office website <https://dst.hkust.edu.hk/eng/detail.php?catid=42&sid=338> .

 

As you embark on your academic journey, we would also like to remind you of the importance of academic integrity - one of HKUST’s core values. Upholding honesty and responsibility in your studies ensures that we all contribute to the excellence and reputation of our university. You can find important information and guidelines in the following resources:

 

*	Undergraduate Student Guide <https://registry.hkust.edu.hk/resource-library/undergraduate-student-guide-0> 
*	Handbook for Research Postgraduate Studies <https://fytgs.hkust.edu.hk/academics/Academic-Regulations-and-Requirements/Handbook-for-Research-Postgraduate-Studies/preface> 
*	Handbook for Taught Postgraduate Studies <https://fytgs.hkust.edu.hk/academics/Academic-Regulations-and-Requirements/Handbook-for-Taught-Postgraduate-Studies/preface> 

 

We are excited to see the campus come alive with your ideas, energy, and enthusiasm. Let us make this year a memorable and inspiring one together!

 

Once again, welcome to HKUST. We wish you every success and happiness in the coming year.

 

 

Best regards,

 

 

Yike Guo, Provost
Jimmy Fung, Associate Provost (Teaching & Learning)
