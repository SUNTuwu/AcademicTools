Workshop



	
Mosaic Alumni Sharing Series - Thriving Academically and Culturally: Postgraduate Life in the UK

	
This alumni-led session offers participants insights into postgraduate study in the UK, including academic expectations, learning styles, and assessments. It also provides tips on adapting to UK life, socializing, handling challenges, and embracing the culture. Whether applying or not, participants will gain practical advice and personal stories to enhance their UK experience. 

Speaker: Alvin Ng <http://www.linkedin.com/in/alvinth>
