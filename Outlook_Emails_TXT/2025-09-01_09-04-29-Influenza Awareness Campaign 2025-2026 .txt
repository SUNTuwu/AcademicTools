Influenza Awareness Campaign 2025-2026

-----------------------------------------------------

 

Exhibition 

Date

8-9 Sept 2025 (Mon-Tue)

Time

12:00noon-4:00pm

Venue

Outside Lam Woo Lecture Theater (LT-B)

 

Pre-enrollment 
There is limited quota for the vaccination. To secure your spot for vaccination, you are required to complete the pre-enrolment.

Date and Time

From 8 Sept 2025 10:00am to 16 Sept 2025 3:00pm

Enrolment Method

1. Pre-enrolment link: https://forms.gle/Qkx9pRePqiheJsfbA <https://forms.gle/Qkx9pRePqiheJsfbA> .
2. A confirmation email will be sent on 18 Sept 2025 to pre-applicants who has successfully been granted with a vaccination quota.

Reminders

    ★  There is limited quota for Flu vaccination, first-come first-served. Only one quota per person, while stocks last.
★  This Flu Vaccination Campaign is applicable to valid HKUST card holders ONLY.

 
Vaccination 
Successful applicants who received the vaccination confirmation e-mail, please bring along your own valid HKUST card, together with HKD$240 cash and receive vaccination at the designated date, time, and venue. 

Date

25-27 Sept 2025 (Thu-Sat)

Time

Thursday to Friday: 10:00am - 4:00pm

Saturday: 9:30am - 11:30am

Venue

LG1 Lobby (Outside S.H. Ho Sports Hall)

Fee

$ 240 per dose (Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine)

Remarks:
1. This designated quadrivalent influenza vaccine is applicable to 6 months old or above. 
2. The special fee is only available during the above schedule dates. Clinic standard price of $350 per dose will be applied out of the schedule dates (Vaccine availability in clinic is dependable on the supply by pharmaceutical firm).
3. The Government “Vaccination Subsidy Scheme 2025/26” is not applicable to the “Influenza Awareness Campaign 2025-26”. For those who are interested in joining the “Vaccination Subsidy Scheme”, please contact HKUST clinic at 2358 6670. 
4. The dates of the campaign may be subject to change under special circumstances and participants will be notified by email.

流感預防活動 2025-2026
--------------------------------

展覽 

日期

2025年9月8至9日(星期一至二)

時間

中午12時至下午4時

地點

林護演講廳(LT-B)外位置

  

預先報名
流感疫苗接種名額有限。為確保您的接種名額，您需要完成預先報名。

日期及時間

2025年9月8日上午10:00 至 2025年9月16日下午3:00 

 報名方法

1. 預先報名網址: https://forms.gle/Qkx9pRePqiheJsfbA <https://forms.gle/Qkx9pRePqiheJsfbA> 。

★ 流感疫苗注射名額有限，先到先得。每人只限一個名額，額滿即止。
★ 是次流感疫苗注射活動只提供予持有有效科大證件之人士。

  

疫苗注射 
成功取得疫苗注射名額之人士收到確認電郵後請於疫苗注射活動之指定日期內帶同閣下有效之科大證件，及港幣 $240現金到指定地點接種疫苗。

日期

2025年9月25-27日 (星期四至六)

時間

星期四至五: 上午10時至下午4時

星期六: 上午9時30分至上午11時30分

地點

LG1大堂 (何善衡體育館外)

費用

每針 $ 240 (四價流感疫苗)

備註
1. 此特定四價流感疫苗只適合六個月或以上之人士接種。 
2. 此優惠價只於本活動之指定日期內有效。任何於本活動指定日期以外之疫苗則以診所正價每針$350計算 (診所疫苗供應需視乎藥廠供貨情況而定)  。
3. 政府推行之「2025/26年度疫苗資助計劃」並不適用於是次「流感預防活動2025-26」。如欲了解更多關於「疫苗資助計劃」之詳情，請向科大診所查詢 (查詢電話: 2358 6670) 。 
4. 如遇特殊情況，活動日期可能會有更改，並將以電郵通知 。
