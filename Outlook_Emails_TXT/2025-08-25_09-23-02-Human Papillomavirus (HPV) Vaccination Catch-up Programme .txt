Human Papillomavirus (HPV) Vaccination Catch-up Programme 

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- 

 

The Department of Health (DH) has launched the Human Papillomavirus (HPV) Vaccination Catch-up Programme in the 2024/25 school year to offer a one-off catch-up HPV vaccination to eligible female Hong Kong residents born between 2004 and 2008. Each eligible person can receive two doses of the vaccine for free. 

  

Eligibility 

i.    Female students studying in the HKUST who were born between 2004 and 2008 and; 

ii.   Hong Kong residents with valid Hong Kong identity card and; 

iii.  Have never received HPV vaccination or have not completed 2-dose schedule of HPV vaccination and;             

iv.   Have already registered or agree to register Electronic Health Record Sharing System (eHealth). 

  

Pre-enrollment 

There is limited quota for the vaccination. To secure your spot for vaccination, you are required to complete the pre-enrolment.      

Date & Time 

From 25 Aug 2025 10:00am to 12 Sept 2025 3:00pm 

Enrolment Method

1. Pre-enrollment link: https://forms.gle/bV91wCmL1kqjy6rN6 <https://forms.gle/bV91wCmL1kqjy6rN6> .

2. Eligible applicant who has successfully enrolled will receive a confirmation email on 16 Sept 2025.  

Reminders 

★  Students who have received 2 doses of HPV vaccination before are deemed as completion of the 2-dose 

     schedule and are therefore not eligible to participate in this HPV Catch-up Programme.

★  Individuals who do not meet the above eligibility criteria but interested in HPV vaccination may 

     participate in the "HPV Disease Prevention Vaccination Campaign", please refer to the relevant poster for 

     details.

  


Vaccination     

Eligible applicant who received the vaccination confirmation e-mail, please bring along your own valid HKID card, valid HKUST student card and vaccination record card (if any), and receive the vaccination at the designated venue during the designated date and time 

9-valent HPV vaccine 

The 1st Dose 

Date 

23-24 Sept 2025 (Tue-Wed)

Time 

10:00am-3:45pm 

Venue 

LG1 Lobby 
(Outside S.H. Ho Sports Hall) 

  

Remarks
1. For immunocompetent females, a 2-dose schedule is recommended, with an interval of 5-13 months between two doses. 

2. The next dose will be scheduled in March 2026. Please check your email for the updated details of the HPV Catch-up Programme.

3. If you fail to present your own valid HKID card and valid HKUST student card on the day of the vaccination, you are not allowed to participate in this programme. Expired/ copy/ electronic copy of HKID card and HKUST student card will not be accepted. 

4. Vaccine will not be reserved if you are unable to receive the vaccination during the designated period. 

5. The dates of the programme may be subject to change under special circumstances and participants will be notified by email. 

6. Should there be any dispute about this programme, the organizer reserves the right to the final decision.

HPV疫苗補種計劃 

----------------------------------------- 

 

衞生署於2024/25學年開展一次性人類乳頭瘤病毒（HPV）疫苗（又稱子宮頸癌疫苗）補種計劃，為出生於2004年至2008年的女性香港居民提供HPV補種。每名合資格人士可免費接種兩劑疫苗。 

  

參與資格 

i.    於2004年至2008年出生並於香港科技大學就讀的香港女學生 及; 

ii.   持有有效香港身分證的香港居民 及; 

iii.  從未接種HPV疫苗 或 尚未完成接種兩劑HPV疫苗 及; 

iv.   已登記 或 同意登記電子健康紀錄互通系統(醫健通) 

  

預先報名 

疫苗接種名額有限。為確保您的接種名額，您需要完成預先報名。

日期及時間 

由2025年8月25日上午10:00 至 2025年9月12日下午3:00  

  

報名方法

1. 預先報名網址: https://forms.gle/bV91wCmL1kqjy6rN6 <https://forms.gle/bV91wCmL1kqjy6rN6> 。 

2. 成功報名之合資格人士將於二零二五年九月十六日收到確定電郵。 

注意事項 

★  曾接種兩劑HPV疫苗的學生將被視為已完成兩劑疫苗接種計劃，因此不符合資格參加是次HPV疫苗補種

     計劃。
★   不符合上述資格而有意接種HPV疫苗之人士，可參加另行舉辦之「預防HPV疾病疫苗注射活動」，詳情

      請參閱相關海報。

  

疫苗注射 

成功取得疫苗注射名額之合資格人士收到確認電郵後請於指定日期及時間內帶同閣下之有效香港身份證，有效科大學生證及疫苗接種記錄卡(如有)到指定地點接種疫苗。  

九合一HPV疫苗 

第一針 

日期 

2025年9月23及24日
(星期二及三) 

時間 

上午10時至下午3時45分 

地點 

LG1大堂 (何善衡體育館外) 



備註 

1. 建議免疫功能正常的女生應接種兩劑疫苗，而兩劑疫苗的接種時間相隔 5-13 個月。

2. 下一劑疫苗注射將定於2026年3月進行，請留意電郵通知以獲取疫苗補種計劃之最新詳情。

3. 若未能於疫苗注射當天出示有效香港身份證及有效科大學生證，則不能參加此計劃。過期證件/證件副本/電子副本恕不接受。 

4. 若未能於指定日期內接種疫苗，疫苗將不獲保留。 

5. 如遇特殊情況，計劃日期可能會有更改，並將以電郵通知。 

6. 如對是次計劃有任何爭議，主辦機構保留最終決定權。 

7. 如欲獲取更多資料，請瀏覽衞生署衞生防護中心網頁: https://www.chp.gov.hk/tc/features/102146.html <https://us-east-2.protection.sophos.com/?d=chp.gov.hk&u=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuY2hwLmdvdi5oay90Yy9mZWF0dXJlcy8xMDIxNDYuaHRtbA==&i=NjFhMGY2MGI0YjgwNTAwZTZiODZiYWRk&t=RURkVWtzZGxRd0txQ1g2anpJeG9KWHV4OTZYc0VNbjBBUk5Kb09XcDkrcz0=&h=b41a1076f2684d718daa213cc4db4b49&s=AVNPUEhUT0NFTkNSWVBUSVYSLtub5sBaIfvXYwRhzi_8thCpgkBxh4JNjPpP_dCZgA> 。
