Date: 25 August 2025 

 

Dear Residents of UG Halls,  

 

Application for Official Hall/ Room Swapping (September 2025) 

 

The Official Hall/ Room Swapping for Residential Year 2025-26 will commence soon, please pay attention to the following details: 

 

Accepted Period:  

From 28 August to 18 September 2025 (Both days inclusive)  

Application Link:  

https://studenthousing.hkust.edu.hk/page/ApplySwap <https://studenthousing.hkust.edu.hk/page/applyswap>  

Application Method: 

https://shrl.hkust.edu.hk/events/hall-room-swapping-residential-year-2025-26-ug <https://shrl.hkust.edu.hk/events/hall-room-swapping-residential-year-2025-26-ug>  

Application Results:  

1. Notification to applicants’ HKUST email 

2. Approved applicants should complete the required procedure at their respective Hall Office on a specific date 

3. Settle hall fee differences via Student Information System (SIS) * 

Points to Note: 

-. All New students are NOTallowed to swap to single room 

-. New students who are under the age of 18 as of 1 September 2025 are NOT allowed to swap to Jockey Club Hall 

-. Exchange students can ONLY swap the SAME room type 

-. Late swapping requests will NOT be considered 

-. If you have no internet access/ no electronic devices, you may come to SHRLO office in person for manual application 

 

*In the case of swapping between different room types, the paid hall fee will be refunded to SIS and may be credited to cover any outstanding fees in the student’s SIS account.  

Failure to complete any part of the swapping procedure by the designated date will result in the automatic withdrawal of the swapping application, or even recovery of hall offer without prior notice, no matter the status of approval. 

 

Official swapping is necessary to maintain a truthful resident record for safety and security purposes. Unauthorized swapping is FORBIDDEN and will lead to disciplinary actions. 

 

Should you have any questions, please email us at ughousing@ust.hk <mailto:ughousing@ust.hk> . Thank you.  

 

 

Best regards, 

Student Housing and Residential Life Office
