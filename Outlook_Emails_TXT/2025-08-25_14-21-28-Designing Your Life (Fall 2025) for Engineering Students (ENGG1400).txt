Designing Your Life



	
Designing Your Life (Fall 2025) for Engineering Students (ENGG1400)

	
Modelled after the highly popular course at Stanford University, this 1-credit course employs a method called “design thinking” to help students from any program develop a constructive and effective approach to finding and designing their life during/after university.

Intended Learning Outcomes:

*	Use design thinking to develop a growth mindset that is an essential foundation for life design.
*	Apply the skills of self-management, reflection, and way finding to chart a personal life and career path.
*	Develop confidence and self-belief to explore different career options.
*	Integrate different planning strategies to design a successful life.
