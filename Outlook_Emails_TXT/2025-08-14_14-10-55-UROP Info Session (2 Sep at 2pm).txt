Dear Professors and Students,

 

The Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program (UROP) is one of the signature programs at HKUST which provides a platform for undergraduate students to engage in academic research across all disciplines under the supervision of faculty members.

 

As announced earlier, we are now calling for projects and applications for Fall 2025–26. To give you more information on the program, including the latest guidelines and policies, the eligibility requirements, project submission and application procedures, awards and sponsorships etc., an Info Session will be held with details as follows:

 

UROP Info Session (face-to-face)

Date: 2 September 2025 (Tuesday)

Time: 2pm – 3pm

Venue: Multi -Purpose Room (Room G019), G/F, Global Graduate Tower (GGT) <https://www.google.com/maps/place/Global+Graduate+Tower+(GGT)/@22.3338937,114.2658369,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m6!3m5!1s0x340405b8095aeeed:0xda844baec0bbfc68!8m2!3d22.3338937!4d114.2658369!16s%2Fg%2F11qhzg9pdr?hl=en&entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI0MDkyNC4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D>  (please expect a 10-minute walk from the Academic Building)

 

All students and faculty members who are interested in UROP are welcome to join the session. Prior registration is required (for students only), and please register here <https://ust.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_esaZXKblkRZh19s>  by 31 August 2025 (Sunday).

 

Should you have any questions, please feel free to contact our office at urop@ust.hk <mailto:urop@ust.hk> . We look forward to seeing you at the Info Session.
