Dear all Engineering UG Students,

 

Invitation to Join HK Electric Green Energy Dreams Come True 2025/26 (Tertiary Institutes)

 

Organised by HK Electric, Green Energy Dreams Come True, is a competition designed to promote research and innovation in green energy, carbon reduction, and sustainability. The programme also recognises outstanding student projects that demonstrate excellence in these areas.

 

Awards

*	Each shortlisted project will receive a seed fund of up to HK$50,000 to support its research and implementation. 
*	The Champion team will be awarded a cash prize of HK$30,000 and a study tour in Mainland China on green energy development. 
*	The 1st and 2nd runner up team will receive a cash prize of HK$20,000 and HK$10,000 respectively.

 

Eligibility

*	The competition is open to higher diploma, associate degree, and bachelor’s degree students from tertiary institutions in Hong Kong. 
*	Students may apply individually or in teams of up to four members.
