Dear colleagues and students,

 

We’re excited to introduce a new Library Material Pickup Station, designed to make borrowing library materials more convenient than ever!

📍 Location: LG1 Outside Learning Commons
🕒 Operating Hours: 24/7



 

From August 25 (Monday), you can authenticate easily using your HKUST card (tap or barcode) or QR code to:

*	Pick up and check-out hold/requested items anytime, day or night
*	Return books at this additional return point

 

Whether you're on a tight schedule or visiting campus outside regular library hours, the Pickup Station ensures you can collect your materials when it suits you best.

We look forward to seeing you make the most of this new service!
