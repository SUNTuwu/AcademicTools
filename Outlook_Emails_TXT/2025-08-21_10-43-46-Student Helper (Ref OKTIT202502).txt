Student Helper (Ref: OKT/IT/2025/02) 

The Office of Knowledge Transfer (OKT) manages intellectual properties arising from HKUST research works and supports faculty and students in their endeavors to pursue university-industry collaboration, entrepreneurship, and technology commercialization. OKT now invites candidates who are interested in Knowledge Transfer to work at the office.

Job Duties:
* Assist in design, development, testing and implementation of internal applications
* Assist in computer setup, configuration and software installation
* Diagnose and troubleshoot hardware and software problems 
* Data entry and documentation
* Perform any other duties as assigned

Requirements:
* Familiar with MS Windows and Microsoft 365 applications
* Knowledge of low-code application and workflow development platforms is preferred 
* Good analytical and communication skills
* Good command of written and spoken English and Chinese
* Previous experience in general administrative work will be an advantage 
* Available work on regular weekdays (8-10 hours/week) in the Fall term 
* Year one to Year three undergraduate

Interested parties please send a DETAILED RESUME with HKDSE results to Ms. Erica Kam by email to ttcac@ust.hk on or before 5 September 2025.  Please quote our reference no �Student Helper (Ref.: OKT/IT/2025/02)� in the subject title of your application.
