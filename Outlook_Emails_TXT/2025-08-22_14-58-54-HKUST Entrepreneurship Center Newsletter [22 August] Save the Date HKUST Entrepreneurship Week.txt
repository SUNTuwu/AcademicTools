Save the Date: HKUST Entrepreneurship Week

 

22 August 2025

 <https://ec.hkust.edu.hk/events/entrepreneurship-week-2025-give-away-and-exhibition-hkust-entrepreneurship-center> 
Welcome back to HKUST!

 

As we kick off a brand new academic year, we’re thrilled to invite you to HKUST Entrepreneurship Week, happening on September 16–17, 2025!

Join us for two vibrant days filled with inspiration, discovery, and connection. Whether you're just starting to explore entrepreneurship or already working on a venture, this is your chance to:

*	Learn about funding, training, and mentorship opportunities from the HKUST Entrepreneurship Center
*	Dive into the GBA innovation ecosystem and explore paths to startup success
*	Connect with like-minded peers, founders, and innovators
*	Discover upcoming programs, competitions, and resources
*	Win exciting prizes through fun, interactive activities!

📍 Event Details:

*	Date: September 16–17, 2025 (Tuesday–Wednesday)
*	Time: 10:30 AM – 3:30 PM
*	Venue: Academic Concourse, HKUST Campus

Whether you're looking to launch an idea, join a team, or simply get inspired, this is the perfect place to start. Let’s build something great—together.

👉 Mark your calendars and don’t miss this chance to be part of HKUST’s growing entrepreneurial community!
