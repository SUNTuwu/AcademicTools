                          THE HONG KONG UNIVERSITY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY
                                                     CENTER FOR LANGUAGE EDUCATION
 
The Center for Language Education is offering Cantonese courses for students in Fall 2025. Whether you are new to Hong Kong or have been part of the HKUST family for a while, if you want to have a faster integration into the local culture, please come and join us! Speaking Cantonese will surely make you feel more at home and help you explore the multiple facets of Hong Kong culture.

Registration:

1.	The CLE online registration system will be open from 9:00 am on 1 September 2025.  Places will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis. 
2.	Fill in an online registration form: https://register.cle.ust.hk/cantonese/.
3.	Pay a deposit [$700] by Visa/Master credit cards.  After successful payment, you will receive an instant message confirming your payment and completion of your registration.
4.	Once a place is confirmed, your registration cannot be withdrawn. Your place and deposit paid are not transferable. 
5.	Participants are required to attend at least 80% of the course in order to enjoy a partial refund of the deposit [$500].  Refund will be arranged after completion of the course.
6.	
	The Center for Language Education has the discretion over the allocation of places and the availability of the course.
