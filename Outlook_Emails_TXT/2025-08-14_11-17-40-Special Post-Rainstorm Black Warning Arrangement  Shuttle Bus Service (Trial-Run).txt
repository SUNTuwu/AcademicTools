主题: Special Post-Rainstorm Black Warning Arrangement : Shuttle Bus Service (Trial-Run)
时间: 2025-08-14 11:17:40
发件人: cso-transportation-news-request@lists.ust.hk
--------------------正文--------------------

Dear All,




This email provides important information about shuttle bus services following the cancellation of Rainstorm Black Warning.




We understand that returning to campus immediately after Rainstorm Black Warning is cancelled can be challenging due to potential transport disruptions. To support you during this time, the university will operate a special post-typhoon shuttle bus service starting from this period as a trial-run.




Service Details:




Routes: Tseung Kwan O and Diamond Hill routes only.




Duration: From 12:10 pm to 1:10 pm (service will run for ONE HOUR ONLY).




Frequency: Buses will run approximately every 5-10 minutes.




Important Notes:




1.  All other regular staff/shuttle bus return-to-campus services remain cancelled during this period.
