Info Session: Non-local Students Working in Hong Kong for UG Students

	
As a non-local student, are you considering working in Hong Kong? What are the policies and procedures that you need to be aware of in order to take up internships or graduate jobs in Hong Kong? Before making a decision, what do you have to consider? What are the policies and procedures that you have to comply before taking up employment in Hong Kong?

This information session is highly recommended for non-local students who wish to work in Hong Kong during and after university studies. Join us to learn the latest updates and get yourself prepared for working in Hong Kong.

Speaker: Kenny Leung <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/quotation.php?qid=13>  (Senior Manager, Career Center)
