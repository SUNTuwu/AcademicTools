Registration as an Undergraduate Student in Fall 2025-26

Welcome to HKUST! After your completion of the JUPAS offer acceptance procedures, you are reminded to complete the following registration procedures by 7 August 2025 (5:00pm), if you have not yet done so.

1) Complete the Online Registration Form	https://registry.hkust.edu.hk/online-reg/jupas	
2) Upload the personal identification and visa documents (if applicable)	
3) Upload your photo for Student Card	
4) Complete the Parent/ Legal Guardian Consent Form for an underage student (for students below 18 years old as of 1 Sep 2025 only)	
5) Complete the Assumption of Risk and Release Form for Enrichment Activities	
6) Activate your HKUST Account	https://myaccount.ust.hk/passwd/studentAcctActivation
(Enquiry: cchelp@ust.hk <mailto:cchelp@ust.hk> )	

Notes:

1.  If you have already completed step 1 to step 5 before, you do not need to do it again. However, you are reminded to activate your HKUST Account. 

2. Your submitted documents will be verified by the Academic Registry which normally takes few working days. Once verified, the status in the Program Registration System will be updated from “In progress” to “Completed”. You will then receive an email entitled "Acknowledgment of Program Registration to the University". 

3. Please visit our FAQ page <https://registry.hkust.edu.hk/resource-library/faq-program-registration-new-undergraduate-students-jupas>  if you have any questions on the registration.

 Student Card (HKUST Card)

Student Cards will be issued to students who have completed the Program Registration from mid-August 2025. The collection schedule will be included in the Acknowledgement of Program Registration email.

Before your collection of Student Card, please ensure that you have uploaded ALL the required documents in the Online Program Registration System <https://registry.hkust.edu.hk/online-reg> .

 

Tuition Fee

Tuition fee for Fall 2025-26 will be posted on the Student Information System (SIS) shortly, and a system-generated email notification will be sent to you when the tuition fees are posted. The tuition due date is 2 September 2025.
