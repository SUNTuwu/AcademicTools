Dear Students, 

 

Welcome to HKUST! We, the iLANG team from the Center for Language Education, are happy to accompany you on your journey at our university. As many of you may be feeling excited while not entirely sure of what to expect in this new learning environment, we are here to support and connect you with the HKUST community.

 

Please join us on the New Student Orientation Day between 11:00 a.m. – 2:30 p.m. on 22 August. You can find our booths at the Atrium in the Academic Building (ground floor, next to the Library).

 

You will have the opportunity to:

  

*	Socialize with our iLANG team members and other HKUST peers from different cultural and academic backgrounds, who will share with you useful tips to excel at HKUST;
*	Enjoy fun language games and activities;
*	Explore our FREE language and culture events and language support services in English, Chinese, and Japanese;  
*	Enjoy the refreshments, collect the souvenirs, and participate in the lucky draw with attractive prizes. 

 

Don't miss this incredible opportunity to kickstart your university life with us! 

 



Regards,
