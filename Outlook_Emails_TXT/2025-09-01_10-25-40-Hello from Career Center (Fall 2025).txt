Welcome (back) to HKUST! Great to see many of you on campus again. Career Center is ready to engage and support you on your journey of career planning, internship and graduate job search. Check out what we have on offer!

 

Career Consultation

 <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?section=meet_advisors> 

Need help with CV writing? Not sure how you can land an internship? Can’t figure out how to navigate a challenging job market? Come meet our career advisors! We are here to help you discuss any career-related issues. We are also excited to announce that our guest career consultants will be available in Sep 2025 to provide career consultation sessions for all of you. Bookings can be made two weeks in advance on our online Consultation Appointment Chart <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/schedule.php> . Walk-ins are also welcome!

Many employers are eager to hire HKUST students! Different types of recruitment talks and events are happening this term. Check them out under Recruitment Events <http://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?sid=47>  and sign up!

 <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?sid=47> 

 

Graduate Jobs and Internships

 <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/job.php> 

Our HKUST-exclusive Job Board <http://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/job.php>  is the one-stop career portal for employers to post their job openings when they look for HKUST students and graduates! You can also visit Joint Institutions Job Information System (JIJIS) <http://www.jijis.org.hk/>  to look for full-time, part-time, temporary and winter/summer jobs, accessible to students from all UGC funded tertiary institutions in Hong Kong. There are also many third-party Internship Programs <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?sid=74>  offering internships across local, mainland and overseas destinations. Many of these opportunities are open to all students, including 1st and 2nd year undergraduates. Check them out!

 

Co-op Program

Career Center offers a university-wide co-op program <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?sid=89> , a unique opportunity to integrate academic learning with hands-on work experience in some of our most popular employers for our undergraduate students. Participating students are required to apply for study leave and take up a full-time co-op placement for a period of at least 4 months. After completion of the co-op internship, students would enroll in a 3-credit co-op course in the following semester. Check out the co-op opportunities on our HKUST-exclusive Job Board <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/job.php?keywords=&EMT%5B%5D=13>  now!

 <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?sid=89>

Credit-Bearing Courses:

1. Career Building Course and 2. Designing Your Life

Career Center offers one-credit general education free electives, Career Building Course <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?sid=51>  (GNED 2510 (T1), GNED 2510 (T2) and SCIE 1100), to students who wish to explore their career paths and equip themselves with the essential skills for job hunting. Undergraduates from School of Engineering can also consider taking Designing Your Life <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?sid=88>  (ENGG 1400) to explore career and life planning using design thinking principles. Enroll via Student Information System (SIS) <https://sisprod.psft.ust.hk/SISPROD/signon.html>  if you are interested.

 <https://career.hkust.edu.hk/web/page.php?sid=51>
