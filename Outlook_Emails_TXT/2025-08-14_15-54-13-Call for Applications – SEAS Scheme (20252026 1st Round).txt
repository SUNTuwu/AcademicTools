Call for Applications – SEAS Scheme (2025/2026 1st Round)



Are you looking for funding support in organizing activities to enrich your student life? Look no further than the Student Enrichment Activities Support Scheme (SEAS Scheme) <https://dst.hkust.edu.hk/eng/detail.php?catid=7&sid=206> ! APPLY NOW.

 

WHO CAN APPLY?

All full-time HKUST students, undergraduates or postgraduates, individuals or groups, are eligible to apply for project funding.

 

OBJECTIVES

It aims to support students and student groups organizing or participating, on a voluntary basis, in activities or projects that promote one or more of the following objectives:

*  Celebrate diversity and inclusivity, and promote multicultural competence

*  Foster a sense of belonging and integrate students within HKUST community

*  Engage students in understanding and serving a wider community

*  Encourage global perspective and awareness 

*  Prioritize holistic development and well-being of students

 

SUPPORT OPTIONS

Successful applicants will receive the following support, together with assistance in event publicity: 

(1)    Eligibility to book and use campus facilities, subject to availability and relevant guidelines of respective offices/departments; and/or

(2)    Student enrichment activities subsidy

 

GUIDELINES & FAQs

Click HERE <https://dst.hkust.edu.hk/eng/detail.php?catid=7&sid=206>  to read through the guidelines and FAQs. 

 

HOW TO APPLY?

Complete the ONLINE APPLICATION FORM <https://ust.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_cu7OaFbAyQVfLYG>  and submit together with an ADVISOR RECOMMENDATION <https://dst.hkust.edu.hk/upload/studentactivities/SEAS_Advisor_Recommendation.pdf>  as well as other relevant information.

 

DEADLINE

Applications for eligibility to book and use campus facilities only: All year round.

Applications requesting activities subsidy: By 14 Sep 2025 (Sunday).

 

CONSULTATION SESSION

Each group of applicants may book a 30-min consultation session within office hours from 18 Aug 2025 to 10 Sep 2025 (except Sat, Sun and public holidays) to ask questions and receive advices from the Secretariat before submission of an application. To book a session, please email to seaf@ust.hk <mailto:seaf@ust.hk>  with your contact information and preferred timeslot(s). We will email to notify you once the consultation session is confirmed.
